## Keyword Gap Analysis

### Methodology

1. Enter your domain + 3-5 top competitors in Ahrefs/SEMrush Keyword Gap tool
2. Filter for "Missing" keywords (competitors rank, you don't)
3. Sort by: traffic potential x keyword difficulty ratio
4. Prioritize: high-traffic, low-difficulty keywords where 2+ competitors rank
5. Check SERP features for each keyword:
   - Does it trigger AI Overviews?
   - Featured snippets present?
   - PAA boxes?
   - Shopping carousel?

### Prioritization Matrix

| Traffic Potential | Keyword Difficulty | Competitors Ranking | Priority |
|------------------|--------------------|---------------------|----------|
| High (1000+/mo) | Low (< 30) | 3+ | P0 — Immediate |
| High (1000+/mo) | Medium (30-60) | 2+ | P1 — This quarter |
| Medium (100-999) | Low (< 30) | 2+ | P1 — This quarter |
| High (1000+/mo) | High (60+) | All | P2 — Long-term investment |
| Low (< 100) | Low (< 30) | 1+ | P3 — Opportunistic |

### AI Overview Gap Analysis (New for 2026)

Only 14% of sources cited in AI Overviews overlap across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google.
Competitive analysis must now include per-platform citation tracking:

1. **Google AI Overviews**: Check Search Console > Search Appearance > AI Overviews
2. **Perplexity**: Query target keywords, note which competitors are cited
3. **ChatGPT Search**: Query target keywords, check citations
4. **Track over time**: Which competitors appear consistently? What content format are they using?

## Backlink Gap Analysis

### Methodology

1. Pull competitor backlink profiles in Ahrefs Site Explorer
2. Filter for DR 40+ linking domains
3. Identify domains linking to 2+ competitors but not you (highest priority targets)
4. Categorize by link type:

| Link Type | Acquisition Strategy | Typical DR |
|-----------|---------------------|------------|
| Editorial mention | Digital PR, data studies | 60-90 |
| Resource page | Outreach + linkable asset | 40-70 |
| Guest post | Pitch + content | 30-60 |
| Directory | Submit listing | 20-50 |
| Forum/comment | Community participation | 10-40 |

5. Build outreach campaign targeting high-overlap domains

### Link Quality Scoring

Not all backlinks are equal. Score prospects:

| Factor | Weight | Best | Worst |
|--------|--------|------|-------|
| Domain Rating | 30% | DR 70+ | DR < 20 |
| Topical relevance | 30% | Same niche | Unrelated |
| Traffic | 20% | 10K+ visits/mo | No traffic |
| Link placement | 10% | In-content, contextual | Footer, sidebar |
| Anchor text | 10% | Natural/branded | Exact-match spam |

## Content Gap Analysis

### What to Look For

1. **Topics competitors cover that you don't** — direct content gaps
2. **Content formats competitors use** — video, infographics, tools, calculators
3. **Content depth** — competitors' word count, heading structure, media usage
4. **Content freshness** — when were competitors' pages last updated?
5. **SERP features captured** — which competitors own featured snippets, PAA?

### How to Execute

```
1. Export competitor sitemaps
2. Categorize their content by topic cluster
3. Map your content against their clusters
4. Identify missing clusters = content gaps
5. Identify thin clusters = depth gaps
6. Prioritize by keyword volume and business value
```

## Link Building Strategies (Ranked by Authority)

### 1. Digital PR / Data Studies (Highest ROI)

Original data studies earn 50-200 links per execution. Process:
1. Analyze proprietary data or conduct original research
2. Extract counterintuitive findings
3. Create a press-ready report with charts
4. Pitch to journalists at relevant publications
5. Follow up within 48 hours

### 2. Expert Quotes (HARO/Connectively Alternatives)

- **Connectively** (formerly HARO) — still active
- **Qwoted** — journalist-expert matching
- **Featured.com** — expert quotes for publications
- **Direct journalist outreach on X/LinkedIn**

**Response speed matters:** first-hour responses convert 60%+ better.

### 3. Guest Posting

Current response rate: ~12% (down from 28% in 2021). Tips:
- Pitch specific angles based on content gaps, not generic articles
- Include a counterintuitive finding or original data point
- Target DR 40+ sites in your niche

**Outreach template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about [their specific article title]

Hi [Name],

Read your piece on [specific topic] — the section on [detail] was solid.

One gap I noticed: [specific gap]. We analyzed [N] [industry] companies
and found [counterintuitive finding]. Would a [X]-word breakdown work
for [their site]?

[Your name]
```

### 4. Broken Link Building

~6% of domains lose links annually. Process:
1. Find competitor pages with 404 errors (Ahrefs > Site Explorer > Best by Links > filter 404)
2. Identify high-authority pages linking to that dead content
3. Create a superior replacement resource
4. Outreach offering your content as the fix

### 5. Resource Page Link Building

Find resource pages: `site:domain.com/resources OR inurl:links "keyword"`
Offer your best linkable asset as an addition.

### 6. Brand Mention Monitoring

Set up Google Alerts and Ahrefs Alerts for your brand name.
When mentioned without a link, reach out — 30-50% conversion rate.

## Natural Link Velocity

| Keyword-rich | ~20% |
